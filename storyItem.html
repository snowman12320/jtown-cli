Kobe bryant - BLACK MAMBA

  • Born｜August 23, 1978
  • Listed height｜6 ft 6 in (1.98 m)

  • NBA draft｜1996 / Round: 1 / Pick: 13th

  • Number｜ 8, 24

The all-time leading scorer in Lakers history, Bryant was the first guard in NBA history to play 20 seasons. His 18 All-Star designations are the second most all time, and he has the most consecutive appearances as a starter. Bryant's four NBA All-Star Game MVP Awards are tied with Bob Pettit for the most in NBA history. He gave himself the nickname "Black Mamba" in the mid-2000s, and the epithet became widely adopted by the general public.

He won gold medals on the 2008 and 2012 U.S. Olympic teams. In 2018, he won the Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film for the film Dear Basketball (2017).
